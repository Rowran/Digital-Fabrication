    • Concept

    Desired Outcome: An artistic wood box with a 3D printed moveable mechanism that opens to reveals a Vitamin D light.

      Technique #1: Moveable Parts
      Technique #2: Onshape/Fusion 360
      Technique #3: Stock Parts
      Technique #4: Laser Cutter
      Technique #5: 3D Printed

    Task Breakdown

  • Creating a Laser Cut Press fit box
  • Raster an Image
  • Cut the Box
  • Create or find a 3D model and use Onshape and/or Fusion 360
  • Print moveable Model parts
  • Using stock parts to attach the 3D printed piece to the Laser Cut Box
  • Insert Vitamin D light into Box
